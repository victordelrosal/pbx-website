What are Pentaborgs?

Pentaborgs represent the five main creator areas: research & analysis, design, development, community & marketing, and management, connecting theory and practice, business and technology.

Pentaborgs can be used as a tool to highlight the connection between research and analysis, design, development and deployment of ideas.

Pentaborgs are based on the Five Innovators Framework used since 2020 to kickstart over 500 project concepts.
